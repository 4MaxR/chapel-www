Chapel is an established project in the [High Performance Software
Foundation](https://hpsf.io) and by extension, the [Linux
Foundation](https://www.linuxfoundation.org/).
{.content-paragraph}

Chapel is governed by a Technical Steering Committee (TSC) whose
topics and meeting notes are [tracked on
GitHub](https://github.com/chapel-lang/TSC).
{.content-paragraph}

Information about the TSC and Chapel's governance can be found in its
[technical
charter](https://github.com/chapel-lang/governance/blob/main/TechCharter.pdf)
and
[GOVERNANCE.md](https://github.com/chapel-lang/governance/blob/main/GOVERNANCE.md)
documents on [GitHub](https://github.com/chapel-lang/governance).
{.content-paragraph}
